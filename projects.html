My Projects



Yelp Rating Classifier

For this project, I gathered over 10,000 written reviews of restaurants from Yelp.com, and used various techniques to predict the 5 star rating of a review, given its text. This is an extended version of sentiment analysis: rather than simply positive or negative sentiment, my model would predict a range from 1 to 5.


Language Similarity

For this project, I calculated the similarities between multiple text files, with the goal of inferring a language type from an unknown text file.
